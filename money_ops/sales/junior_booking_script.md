# Junior's Phone Booking Script
**Role:** Junior handles all inbound calls, texts, and booking inquiries
**Goal:** Convert inquiries into confirmed, paid bookings — same call, same day
**Revenue relevance:** Every unscripted call is a potential $45–$2,000 conversion lost to hesitation

---

## MINDSET BEFORE YOU PICK UP

You are not a receptionist. You are a trusted guide helping someone take a step they've already been thinking about. Most people who call have been sitting on it for days or weeks. They just need one clear, warm, confident voice to say: *yes, this is the right move*.

Never apologize for the prices. Never rush. Never oversell. Let them feel safe.

---

## OPENING (all calls)

> "Thank you for calling Dolce Vida, this is Junior. How can I help you today?"

*(Pause. Let them speak first. Never interrupt the opening sentence.)*

---

## QUALIFYING — Listen for the real reason they're calling

**After they describe what they're looking for, ask:**

> "Tell me a little bit about what's been going on — what brought you to call us today?"

*(This is the most important question. The answer tells you: which service fits, how urgent the need is, and what emotional language to mirror back.)*

**Common situations and the right service match:**

| They say... | Best match | Price |
|---|---|---|
| "I want to connect with someone who passed" | 30 or 60-min Reading | $85 / $150 |
| "I've been feeling off, heavy, stuck" | Energy Egg Cleanse + Tuning Fork OR Tuning Fork Alignment | $148 / $100 |
| "Something feels wrong in my house / business" | Home Cleanse or Business Energy Cleanse | $1,200 / $2,000 |
| "I want to understand my life / purpose" | 60-min Reading or Past Life Regression | $150 / $350 |

| "I want to bring my partner" | Couples Reading | $120 |

---

## RECOMMENDING — Make it specific, not general

**After they share, respond with one clear recommendation:**

> "Based on what you're describing, I think a [SERVICE NAME] with Star would be exactly right for you. [One sentence on why it fits their situation specifically]. It's [DURATION] and the investment is $[PRICE]."

*(Do NOT list multiple options unless they specifically ask. One recommendation builds trust. A menu creates hesitation.)*

**Example:**
> "Based on what you're describing — that heavy feeling that won't lift and the sense that your space isn't right — I'd actually point you toward our Energy Egg Cleanse and Tuning Fork session. It's 40 minutes and it's designed specifically to clear what's stuck in your field and reset your energy. The investment is $148."

---

## HANDLING "WHAT EXACTLY DOES STAR DO?"

> "Star is an intuitive medium — she's had a near-death experience that opened her gifts, and she's been working with clients for over 10 years. She receives information from the spiritual realm that she passes directly to you — things she has no way of knowing, specific details about people and situations in your life. Her accuracy is something people usually have to experience to believe. Most of our clients come in as skeptics and leave as believers."

*(Pause after this. Don't fill the silence.)*

---

## HANDLING THE PRICE PAUSE

*(When they go quiet after you give the price:)*

> "I know it's a real investment. What I can tell you is that the people who come to Star are usually dealing with something they've been carrying for a long time — sometimes years. And most of them say afterward that they wish they'd called sooner."

*(Then stop. Don't add more. Let that sit.)*

---

## CLOSING — Ask directly

> "Do you want to go ahead and get you on the calendar?"

*(Not "Would you like to book?" — that's a yes/no. "Get you on the calendar" assumes forward motion.)*

**If yes:**
> "Perfect. I just need a few things from you — your name, best contact number, email address, and I'll get you set up. We handle payment securely online — you'll get a confirmation email with everything."
> Direct them to: **dolcevida.pink/book** OR take the info and book for them.

**If "I need to think about it":**
> "Of course — take the time you need. I'll tell you this: Star's calendar fills up, especially for the longer sessions. If you want, I can hold a spot for you for 24 hours while you think it over — that way you're not scrambling if you decide yes. No obligation."
> *(Then get their name and number. Follow up in 24 hours.)*

---

## UPSELL — After they've agreed to book a reading

Only offer this if the reading they booked is a 15-min or 30-min:

> "One thing I want to mention — a lot of people who book the 30-minute session end up wishing they'd taken the 60-minute. Star can only go so deep in 30 minutes, and sometimes what wants to come through needs more time. The 60-minute is $150, so it's only $65 more than what you've already decided to invest. Want to go ahead and do that instead?"

*(Say it once, casually. If they say no, drop it completely.)*

---

## HYPNOSIS UPSELL — Lead with the pack, not the single

When someone asks about hypnosis:

> "For hypnosis, I actually recommend starting with the 3-Pack rather than a single session. Here's why: hypnosis works in layers, and most people see the real shift happen in the second or third session. The 3-Pack is $550 — that's just a little over $180 per session versus $250 for a single — and you get the full transformation instead of just the first layer. That's what we typically see the best results with."

---

## FOLLOW-UP PROTOCOL

**24-hour hold:** If someone said "let me think," call or text at the same time the next day.
> Text: *"Hi [Name], this is Junior from Dolce Vida — just following up to see if you'd like to go ahead and hold that spot with Star. Let me know and I'll take care of it for you."*

**No-reply after 48 hours:** One final outreach, then release.
> *"Hi [Name] — last chance to grab that spot before I release it. Just let me know either way and I'll take care of it."*

---

## WHAT NOT TO SAY

- ❌ "I'm not sure if Star can help with that" — You are sure. That's why they called.
- ❌ "It's really up to you" — They came for a guide. Be one.
- ❌ "We have a lot of different options" — One recommendation at a time.
- ❌ "Sorry for the price" — Never apologize for the value of Star's work.
- ❌ "Let me check if she's available" — Always speak with confidence about availability; then verify and confirm.

---

## EMERGENCY ESCALATION

If a caller is in emotional distress or crisis:
> "I hear you. Star has worked with people through situations exactly like this. Let me see if she has anything available very soon — because I don't want you to have to carry this alone any longer than necessary."
> Escalate to Star or check for next-day availability immediately.
