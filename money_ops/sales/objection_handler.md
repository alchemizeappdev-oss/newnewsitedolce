# High-Ticket Objection Handler
**For:** Junior (phone/text) and Star (in-person)
**Focus:** $100–$2,000 services — Rituals, Healing Packages, Hex Removal, Cleanses
**Revenue relevance:** The difference between a $85 reading and a $1,500 Hex Removal is one handled objection

---

## THE RULE BEFORE YOU READ THIS

Objections are not rejections. They are questions in disguise. Every objection means the person is still engaged. If they had decided no, they would have already hung up.

Your job is not to argue. Your job is to understand what's underneath the objection and meet it there.

---

## OBJECTION 1: "It's too expensive / I can't afford that right now"

**The real meaning:** *"Help me feel certain enough that I'll find the money."*

**Response (Standard):**
> "I completely understand. Can I ask — how long have you been dealing with [what they described]?"
> *(Let them answer.)*
> "So you've been carrying this for [X months / years]. What has that cost you — in stress, in sleep, in relationships, in missed opportunities? Sometimes the most expensive thing we do is wait."

**Response (Ritual / High-ticket):**
> "The Fire Cleanse / Hex Removal / Business Cleanse is a significant investment, you're right. Most people who book it have tried other things first — and they're calling us because those things didn't hold. Star's work is different because it goes to the root, not just the surface. The people who've done it describe it as the thing that finally worked. I can't promise you that — but that is consistently what we hear."

**If they push back again:**
> "Is there a version of this that works for your budget right now? We could start with a reading or a Tuning Fork session and see what Star identifies — sometimes that points to exactly what's needed and at what investment makes sense."
*(Never offer a discount. Offer a smaller entry-point service instead.)*

---

## OBJECTION 2: "I'm not sure I believe in this / I'm a skeptic"

**The real meaning:** *"I'm afraid to hope and be disappointed."*

**Response:**
> "That's actually the most honest thing someone can say before coming to Star. She works with skeptics all the time — and honestly, she prefers it. When she tells you something she has no way of knowing, it lands harder when you came in not believing."

> "We don't ask you to believe anything going in. We just ask you to come with an open mind — not an open conclusion. Give yourself one session and see what happens. The 30-minute reading is the lowest-risk way to do that."

**If they mention a bad experience with someone else:**
> "I'm sorry you had that experience. There are a lot of people who call themselves mediums or healers, and not all of them are genuine. What I can tell you is that Star has been doing this for over 10 years, she has 500+ clients, and she consistently tells people specific things she has no way of accessing. We're happy to let the session speak for itself."

---

## OBJECTION 3: "I need to think about it / talk to my partner"

**The real meaning:** *"I want to say yes but I don't feel certain enough yet" OR "I need permission."*

**Response (thinking about it):**
> "Of course — take whatever time you need. I just want to mention that Star's calendar does fill up, especially for the longer sessions and rituals. I can hold a spot for you for 24 hours so you're not scrambling. No charge, no obligation. If you decide it's not the right time, just let me know and I'll release it."

**Response (talking to partner):**
> "Absolutely. When you talk to them — what would help them understand why you want to do this? Sometimes it helps to have a clear answer ready. Is there something specific you're hoping to get out of the session that they might connect with?"
*(Help them build their own case. They'll use the same words on their partner.)*

> "Also — couples readings are something we offer. If they're open to it, coming together can actually be more powerful than either of you going alone."

---

## OBJECTION 4: "Can I just start with something small and see?"

**The real meaning:** *"Help me trust before I invest more."*

**Response:**
> "Yes — and that's actually a great approach. The 30-minute reading is the perfect starting point. It gives Star enough time to connect and give you real, specific information — and it gives you a chance to experience her work firsthand. A lot of our long-term clients started exactly where you are."

*(This is not a problem — this is a win. Book the $85 reading with full conviction. A client who experiences Star once usually comes back for more.)*

**The subtle ask after booking the small session:**
> "One thing I'll mention — if you do feel called to do the deeper work during or after your reading, Star may point you in that direction. Just know you're always free to decide then. The reading is its own complete thing."

---

## OBJECTION 5: "What if it doesn't work / what's your guarantee?"

**The real meaning:** *"I'm afraid of wasting money on something that lets me down."*

**Response:**
> "That's a fair question. Here's what I can honestly tell you: Star doesn't promise specific outcomes, and any genuine healer or medium will tell you the same thing. What she can promise is that she brings everything she has to every single session — her full gift, her full presence, her full care. She is not performing. She is actually doing this work."

> "Our policy: if you don't feel the session delivered value, we'd want to know about it. Star has been doing this for over a decade and the overwhelming experience her clients report is transformation — not disappointment. But we're human. If something doesn't land, we'd rather you tell us than walk away feeling like it wasn't worth it."

*(Do not offer refunds proactively. This response builds trust without creating a liability.)*

---

## OBJECTION 6: "Is this safe? / Will this interfere with my religion?"

**The real meaning:** *"I'm afraid of doing something against my values or beliefs."*

**Response:**
> "That's an important question and I'm glad you asked it. Star works from a place of love, light, and divine connection. She was guided by Archangel Michael during her near-death experience — her entire practice is rooted in a relationship with the divine, not against it. She has deeply devout Christian, Catholic, and other faith-tradition clients. The work doesn't ask you to change what you believe — it simply opens a channel for guidance and healing."

> "If you'd like, I can have Star speak with you briefly before you book — sometimes hearing it in her own words makes the difference."

## POWER PHRASES (use sparingly, at the right moment)

| Situation | Phrase |
|---|---|
| After listing the price | *"Most people say they wish they'd called sooner."* |
| When they're close to saying yes | *"Let's just get you on the calendar."* |
| When they mention how long they've suffered | *"You've been carrying this long enough."* |
| When they're afraid it won't work | *"The session will tell you what you need to know."* |
| When they're almost convinced | *"Your gut brought you to this call. That's not an accident."* |

---

## WHAT NEVER TO DO

- ❌ Drop the price or offer a discount — devalues Star's gift permanently
- ❌ Promise specific results — this is energy work, not a vending machine
- ❌ Argue with their skepticism — it creates walls, not connections
- ❌ Panic when they pause — silence is the objection working itself out
- ❌ List every service when they hesitate — one clear path forward, always
