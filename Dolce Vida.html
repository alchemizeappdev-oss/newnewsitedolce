The Story

She died.
And that is when everything began.

There was a moment — a split second that should have been the end — when Star Monreal left this world. No warning. No goodbye. Just silence, then light, then something no human language was built to describe.

Star Monreal

She came back
with a transmission.

She stood beyond the veil — not in darkness, not in fear — in a peace so complete it erased every question she had ever carried. In a love so vast it made every wound, every heartbreak, every year of feeling lost suddenly make sense.

In the presence of the Creator and Archangel Michael, she was shown what most people spend their entire lives searching for: truth, purpose, and the real reason the soul chooses to come here. And then she came back.

Waking up was not a relief. It was a reckoning. She returned knowing that people are living far beneath their power. That spirits are exhausted, energy is blocked, intuition is buried, and people have forgotten who they really are. That is why Dolce Vida was born.

JR

He was born
to protect it.

JR comes from a small town, but his story was never meant to stay small. Through his mother's bloodline, he carries the gifts of his grandfather — a medicine man, a shaman, a healer. The same energy lives in him.

JR does not need cards to know what others are feeling. His intuition is immediate, powerful, undeniable. There was a woman preparing for surgery on her hand. After working with JR, she canceled the surgery completely. Others have come to him carrying pain, fear, confusion, even illness — and left changed.

He is fearless. Wild. Drawn to the edge. He loves motocross, snowboarding, speed, adrenaline. He carries both chaos and calm, intensity and healing. The same man who can race toward danger is the man who can sit in silence and help someone find themselves again.
